Proactive memory layer

Your notes.
When you need them.

Jot watches your active app and surfaces the right note in a small overlay when you switch context. Everything is stored locally on your Mac; AI helpers are optional if you add your own API key.

Download for macOS

Why Jot

⚡
Proactive, not reactive
Jot detects your active app and surfaces relevant notes automatically — no search required.
🔒
Local-first
Notes and attachments live in SQLite and on disk. No account required. Optional AI calls Anthropic only when you configure a key.
⌘
Keyboard-first
⌘P opens search from anywhere. ⌘N opens capture from the main window. Snooze or dismiss overlays from the keyboard.
🧠
Gets smarter
Notes you open get ranked higher next time. The more you use it, the more accurate the surfacing.

How it works

1
Capture a thought
Open search (⌘P), then capture (⌘N). Link to an app or use “remind me this: … when i open this …”.
2
Switch to that app
Jot watches your workflow. When you open Slack, Zoom, VS Code — it knows.
3
Note appears instantly
A beautiful overlay surfaces your most relevant notes — right when you need them.
4
Never lose context
Open it, snooze it, or mark it done. Your memory layer works silently in the background.

Built for knowledge workers

PMs, engineers, founders, researchers — anyone who switches between 5+ apps a day and can't afford to lose context.
